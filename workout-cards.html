Recovery Day 6 — Active Recovery / Sport Day
30-60 min

    Feeling good → Casual tennis, easy bike, shoot hoops
    Keep it fun and light. 30-45 min.
  • B
    Feeling beat → 20-min walk + 15-min yoga

  • C
    Full rest → Light foam rolling + sauna
    Recovery IS training. Don't feel guilty.
Phase 2

Performance — Weeks 5-8

Phase 2 Changes

Compound lifts → heavier, fewer reps (5×5, 5×3)
Walking Lunges → Jump Lunges (3×8 ea)
Leg Press → Box Jumps (4×5)
Plank → 45-60 sec | Knee Raise → Leg Raise
+ Med Ball Slams (3×8) on Day 4
+ Band Lateral Shuffles (3×30s) on Day 4
